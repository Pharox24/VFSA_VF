About Us

VFSA is a dynamic and expanding company committed to bridging the gap between labor market needs and skilled Egyptian workers. Since its inception, VFSA has been dedicated to responsibly preparing and supplying workers based on realistic assessments of labor demands in various sectors. Founded by a team with substantial international experience, the company possesses a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced by migrant workers, along with the expectations of overseas employers. Our comprehensive service approach begins with a meticulous process of worker selection and extends to ongoing support during early employment stages. This commitment ensures that each worker not only meets employer standards but also receives support in adapting to their new roles, fostering a smooth transition and mutual satisfaction. Our team, trained in all aspects of recruitment, selection, and follow-up, diligently oversees each phase, reflecting our promise to support both workers and employers alike in a sustainable and impactful manner.
